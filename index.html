About First Class Legal

First Class Legal is a leading Australian law firm specializing in property conveyancing and related legal services. Operating across Victoria and Queensland, the firm has recently expanded with new offices on the Sunshine Coast and Gold Coast.

With a focus on professionalism, efficiency, and client satisfaction, First Class Legal handles thousands of settlements each year. As a full legal practice (not just a conveyancing service), they offer greater security and can manage contentious matters in-house without referrals.

They pioneered electronic conveyancing in Australia, becoming the first firm to lodge 1,000 transfers electronically in 2018. Their innovative app allows clients to track progress, download documents, and stay informed throughout the process.
